About AvestAI

Making powerful, privacy-first AI accessible to everyone

AvestAI is a boutique technology service specializing in personal AI assistant setup for Apple users.

We believe AI should work for you — not the other way around. But setting up a powerful AI assistant like OpenClaw requires technical expertise most people don't have (and shouldn't need).

That's where we come in.

Our Story

Founder: Chance Parker

Chance is a commercial pilot, flight instructor, and aviation business owner with over 30 years in the home systems technology business. After years of juggling flight schedules, weather monitoring, maintenance tracking, and business operations, he discovered OpenClaw — an open-source personal AI platform.

The problem? Setting it up took 20+ hours of technical work.

"I thought: If I'm struggling with this, and I've been in tech for decades, what about everyone else?"

That's when AvestAI was born.

Our Mission

Make powerful, privacy-first AI accessible to everyone — especially busy professionals who want their technology to just work.
