# Sophia's Airpark Adventures
## Season 1, Episode 1: "The New Hangar" (PILOT)

**Runtime:** 10-12 minutes  
**Target Age:** 3-6 years  
**Theme:** Rules exist for reasons you might not see yet

---

## CHARACTERS INTRODUCED

- **SOPHIA** (6) — Curious, energetic Filipina girl with long dark hair, round face, big brown eyes. Quick to explore, slow to think first.
- **PIPER** — Sophia's scruffy tan mutt. Expressive ears, always by her side.
- **MOM (ANNA)** — Warm, organized Filipina mother. Gentle but firm.
- **DAD (BILL)** — Calm, handy pilot. Wears a baseball cap, has kind eyes.
- **GUS** — Retired pilot, 70s, white beard, always has a story. Lives three hangars down.

---

## COLD OPEN

### SCENE 1: MORNING AT HOME
**Location:** Sophia's house — kitchen and back porch  
**Time:** Early morning, golden light

**VISUAL:** A cozy home with airplane decorations. Through the window, we see hangars and a grass runway in the distance. A yellow Piper Cub sits near one hangar.

*SOPHIA (V.O.)*  
*(excited, narrating)*  
My name is Sophia, and I live in the best place in the whole wide world...

**VISUAL:** Camera pushes through the window, revealing the airpark — lakes sparkling, a golf cart puttering by, deer grazing near the runway.

*SOPHIA (V.O.)*  
...an airpark! That means airplanes live here too. Just like us!

**CUT TO:** Kitchen. ANNA is making breakfast. SOPHIA bursts in, PIPER at her heels.

*SOPHIA*  
Mom! Mom! I heard an airplane landing! Can I go see?

*ANNA*  
*(smiling, flipping a pancake)*  
Good morning to you too, anak. Breakfast first, adventures second.

*SOPHIA*  
*(bouncing on her toes)*  
But what if it's a NEW airplane? What if I miss it?

*ANNA*  
Then it will still be there after pancakes.

**VISUAL:** Sophia slumps dramatically into her chair. Piper mirrors her, slumping onto the floor.

*SOPHIA*  
*(to Piper)*  
She doesn't understand, Piper. Airplanes don't wait.

*PIPER*  
*(whines sympathetically)*

---

## ACT ONE: THE RULES

### SCENE 2: AFTER BREAKFAST
**Location:** Back porch, view of hangars  
**Time:** Morning

**VISUAL:** Sophia scrapes her plate clean in record time. She grabs Piper's leash.

*SOPHIA*  
Done! Piper and I are going exploring!

*ANNA*  
*(wiping hands on towel)*  
Stay where I can find you. Don't go past Gus's hangar. And Sophia—

*SOPHIA*  
*(already halfway out the door)*  
I know, I know!

*ANNA*  
*(calling after her)*  
Stay away from the old gray hangar at the end. It's not safe.

**VISUAL:** Sophia waves without looking back. Anna watches from the porch, a hint of worry on her face.

---

### SCENE 3: EXPLORING THE AIRPARK
**Location:** Airpark grounds — taxiway, hangars, golf cart path  
**Time:** Morning

**VISUAL:** Montage of Sophia and Piper exploring:
- Waving at MR. JENKINS washing his Cessna
- Petting a horse at the fence line
- Watching a golf cart full of laughing pilots putter by
- Spotting deer near the tree line (Piper barks, they bounce away)

*SOPHIA*  
*(to Piper, running)*  
Come on, let's see who landed!

**VISUAL:** They reach GUS's hangar. GUS is in a lawn chair, cup of coffee, watching the world go by.

*GUS*  
*(calling out)*  
Well, well! If it isn't the Airpark Explorer and her trusty co-pilot!

*SOPHIA*  
*(stopping, catching her breath)*  
Hi Gus! Did you see the airplane that landed?

*GUS*  
Sure did. Pretty little Bonanza. Came in smooth as butter on a biscuit.

*SOPHIA*  
Where is it? Where is it?

*GUS*  
*(pointing)*  
Down at the visitor tie-downs. Past my place, before the old gray hangar.

**VISUAL:** Sophia looks past Gus's hangar. We see the visitor area... and beyond it, a weathered GRAY HANGAR, doors slightly open, mysterious.

*SOPHIA*  
*(eyes fixed on the gray hangar)*  
What's in that old hangar, Gus?

*GUS*  
*(sipping coffee, casual)*  
Nothing good for little explorers. That old building's been falling apart for years. Stay clear of it, Sophia.

*SOPHIA*  
But WHY?

*GUS*  
*(chuckling)*  
Same reason you don't pet a cactus. Some things look interesting but ain't worth the trouble.

**VISUAL:** Sophia nods, but her eyes linger on the gray hangar. Piper tugs toward the visitor area.

*SOPHIA*  
Okay... Come on, Piper!

**VISUAL:** She runs toward the visitor tie-downs. But she keeps glancing back at the gray hangar.

---

### SCENE 4: THE BONANZA
**Location:** Visitor tie-downs  
**Time:** Late morning

**VISUAL:** A shiny Beechcraft Bonanza sits gleaming in the sun. A PILOT is unloading a bag. Sophia watches from a respectful distance.

*SOPHIA*  
*(whispering to Piper)*  
It's so pretty...

*PILOT*  
*(noticing her, friendly)*  
You like airplanes, huh?

*SOPHIA*  
*(nodding vigorously)*  
My dad has a yellow one! It's called a Piper Cub.

*PILOT*  
That's a classic! Your dad's got good taste.

**VISUAL:** The pilot waves and heads toward the restaurant. Sophia watches him go, then turns... and sees the gray hangar again. The doors have swung open a bit more in the breeze. Inside, she can see the shadowy shape of... something.

*SOPHIA*  
*(to Piper, whispering)*  
I just want to see what's inside. Just a peek.

*PIPER*  
*(whines, ears back)*

*SOPHIA*  
We won't GO in. We'll just look.

---

## ACT TWO: THE MISTAKE

### SCENE 5: THE GRAY HANGAR — OUTSIDE
**Location:** Old gray hangar, exterior  
**Time:** Late morning

**VISUAL:** Sophia approaches slowly. The hangar is weathered, paint peeling. A faded "KEEP OUT" sign hangs crooked on the door. Weeds grow around the foundation.

*SOPHIA*  
*(to herself)*  
It's just an old building. What's so dangerous about that?

**VISUAL:** She peeks through the gap in the doors. Inside: dusty tarps, old wooden crates, the skeleton of an old airplane frame. Shafts of light cut through holes in the roof.

*SOPHIA*  
*(gasping)*  
Piper, look! An airplane! A real one!

**VISUAL:** Piper hangs back, whimpering.

*SOPHIA*  
*(stepping through the gap)*  
I'll just look for one second...

---

### SCENE 6: THE GRAY HANGAR — INSIDE
**Location:** Inside the old hangar  
**Time:** Late morning

**VISUAL:** Sophia moves through the dusty space, eyes wide. The old airplane frame is beautiful in a haunted way. Fabric hangs in tatters. 

*SOPHIA*  
*(touching the wing frame gently)*  
I wonder who flew you...

**SOUND:** CREEEEAK. A gust of wind. The big doors shift.

**VISUAL:** Sophia spins around. The doors have blown partially closed. Less light now.

*SOPHIA*  
*(nervous)*  
Okay Piper, let's go—

**VISUAL:** She looks for Piper. He's outside, pacing, whining through the gap.

*SOPHIA*  
Piper! Come on!

**VISUAL:** She starts toward the door. Steps on a rotted floorboard—

**SOUND:** CRACK!

**VISUAL:** Her foot goes through! She falls, catching herself, but her leg is stuck between broken boards.

*SOPHIA*  
*(scared)*  
Ow! Piper! PIPER!

**VISUAL:** She pulls. Her foot is wedged tight. The more she struggles, the tighter it feels.

*SOPHIA*  
*(tears forming)*  
Help! Somebody help!

**VISUAL:** Piper barks frantically outside. Sophia is stuck. Dust swirls in the dim light. She's alone.

---

### SCENE 7: STUCK
**Location:** Inside the old hangar  
**Time:** Midday

**VISUAL:** Time passes. Light shifts through the roof holes. Sophia has stopped struggling. She sits awkwardly, leg still stuck, wiping tears.

*SOPHIA*  
*(quietly, to herself)*  
Mom said stay away. Gus said stay away. 
*(voice breaking)*  
Why didn't I listen?

**VISUAL:** She looks at the old airplane frame.

*SOPHIA*  
I just wanted to see...

**VISUAL:** Piper has squeezed through the gap. He approaches carefully, sniffing the broken boards around Sophia.

*SOPHIA*  
*(small smile through tears)*  
Hi Piper. You came.

**VISUAL:** Piper licks her face. She hugs him carefully.

*SOPHIA*  
I'm sorry I didn't listen to you either.

**SOUND:** In the distance — a voice calling.

*BILL (O.S.)*  
Sophia! SOPHIA!

*SOPHIA*  
*(hope surging)*  
DAD! I'M IN HERE! IN THE OLD HANGAR!

---

### SCENE 8: THE RESCUE
**Location:** Old hangar  
**Time:** Midday

**VISUAL:** The hangar doors swing open. BILL rushes in, worry on his face, followed by ANNA. Light floods the space.

*ANNA*  
*(gasping)*  
Sophia!

*BILL*  
*(quickly assessing, calm)*  
Hold still, sweetheart. Let me see.

**VISUAL:** Bill kneels, examines the broken boards. Carefully, he widens the gap and slides Sophia's foot free. Her shoe is scuffed, her ankle sore, but she's okay.

*SOPHIA*  
*(crying, hugging him)*  
I'm sorry! I'm sorry! I just wanted to look!

*BILL*  
*(holding her, voice steady)*  
I know. I know.

*ANNA*  
*(kneeling, brushing hair from Sophia's face)*  
Are you hurt, anak?

*SOPHIA*  
*(sniffling)*  
My foot. And my feelings.

**VISUAL:** Despite everything, Anna almost smiles.

---

## ACT THREE: THE LESSON

### SCENE 9: BACK HOME — PORCH
**Location:** Sophia's house — back porch  
**Time:** Afternoon, golden light

**VISUAL:** Sophia sits on the porch steps, ankle wrapped in a small bandage. Piper lies beside her. Bill sits next to her, both looking out at the airpark.

*SOPHIA*  
Dad? Are you mad at me?

*BILL*  
*(thoughtful pause)*  
I was scared. That's different.

*SOPHIA*  
But I broke the rule.

*BILL*  
Why do you think we made that rule?

*SOPHIA*  
*(looking at her ankle)*  
Because... the floor is broken?

*BILL*  
That's part of it. What else?

*SOPHIA*  
*(thinking)*  
Because... you couldn't see me if something went wrong?

*BILL*  
*(nodding)*  
And?

*SOPHIA*  
*(slowly)*  
Because... you knew it was dangerous and I didn't. Even though I thought I did.

**VISUAL:** Bill puts his arm around her.

*BILL*  
Rules aren't just "no," Sophia. They're shortcuts. Someone learned the hard way so you don't have to.

*SOPHIA*  
*(quietly)*  
I learned the hard way today.

*BILL*  
*(gentle)*  
You did. But you're okay. That's what matters.

---

### SCENE 10: SOPHIA'S REFLECTION
**Location:** Sophia's bedroom  
**Time:** Evening

**VISUAL:** Sophia is in bed, Piper curled at her feet. Anna sits on the edge, tucking her in.

*ANNA*  
Big day, anak.

*SOPHIA*  
Mom? I'm sorry I didn't listen.

*ANNA*  
*(kissing her forehead)*  
I know. I forgive you.

*SOPHIA*  
How did Dad find me?

*ANNA*  
Piper came running home, barking his head off. He led us right to you.

**VISUAL:** Sophia looks at Piper with new appreciation.

*SOPHIA*  
He saved me.

*ANNA*  
You both saved each other. He tried to warn you, and you finally listened.

**VISUAL:** Sophia hugs Piper tight.

*SOPHIA*  
*(to Piper)*  
Next time you tell me something's a bad idea, I'll listen. Promise.

*PIPER*  
*(tail wag, licks her cheek)*

---

### SCENE 11: CLOSING
**Location:** Sophia's bedroom / Airpark (montage)  
**Time:** Night / Next morning

**VISUAL:** Anna turns off the light. Sophia stares at the ceiling, thinking.

*SOPHIA (V.O.)*  
*(narrating)*  
I learned something today. When someone who loves you says "stay away," they're not just being mean. They probably know something you don't.

**VISUAL:** Morning. Sophia runs outside. She waves at Gus.

*GUS*  
*(grinning)*  
Heard you had quite an adventure yesterday!

*SOPHIA*  
*(embarrassed but smiling)*  
I should have listened to you.

*GUS*  
*(winking)*  
That's okay. Some of us have to touch the cactus to believe it's pokey.

**VISUAL:** Sophia laughs. She runs off with Piper — but this time, she stops at the edge of Gus's hangar and looks back at the gray building.

*SOPHIA (V.O.)*  
I still want to know everything about everything. That's who I am. But maybe... I'll ask more questions first.

**VISUAL:** She turns and runs toward home, Piper bounding beside her.

*SOPHIA (V.O.)*  
And LISTEN to the answers.

**VISUAL:** Wide shot of the airpark — golden morning light, a plane taking off in the distance, deer grazing, Sophia and Piper running home.

---

## END OF EPISODE

**CREDITS ROLL** over scenes of the airpark — golf carts, hangars, the runway, horses by the fence.

# PRODUCTION NOTES

**Key Animation Moments:**
- Sophia's hair bouncing as she runs
- Piper's expressive ears (up when happy, back when worried)
- The contrast between bright airpark and dim hangar interior
- Dust particles in light beams inside hangar
- Sophia's tears (should feel real, not cartoonish)

**Sound Design:**
- Airplane engines in distance (constant ambient)
- Birds chirping in morning scenes
- Creaking wood in hangar
- Piper's various whines and barks (emotional communication)

**Music Cues:**
- Bright, adventure theme for exploring
- Tension builds approaching hangar
- Quiet, sparse during stuck scene
- Warm resolution theme for porch conversation
