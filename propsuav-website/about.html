About PropsUAV

Trusted expertise, proven certifications, local service

Chance Parker, Founder

Chance Parker brings decades of aviation expertise and a passion for agricultural innovation to PropsUAV. With a background in commercial aviation, aircraft maintenance, and now agricultural technology, Chance has built PropsUAV on a foundation of safety, professionalism, and customer-focused service.

Based in Hilltop Lakes, Texas, PropsUAV serves Central Texas farmers with precision crop spraying solutions backed by FAA certification and professional-grade equipment. Whether you're managing pests, controlling weeds, preventing disease, or optimizing nutrition, Chance brings expert knowledge and meticulous attention to detail to every operation.

PropsUAV is part of Avest Ltd, a portfolio of aviation and agricultural businesses dedicated to supporting farmers and advancing agricultural technology in Texas.
