Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the form on this page or call us directly at (936) 444-2869. We'll assess your needs and provide a customized quote within 24 hours.

What chemicals can you apply?

We can apply any FAA-approved agricultural chemicals, including pesticides, herbicides, fungicides, and fertilizers. You provide the products, or we can recommend suitable options based on your needs.

How much area can you cover?

With the DJI Agras T40, we can cover up to 50 acres per day depending on field conditions, application width, and tank refill frequency. Larger operations may require multiple days or staging.

What are your service areas?

We primarily serve Central Texas, including Hilltop Lakes, Leon County, and surrounding areas. Call to confirm availability for your location.

Do you provide night operations?

Yes. We have FAA authorization for night operations. This is especially useful for temperature-sensitive applications or when daytime conditions aren't ideal.

Are you fully insured?

Yes. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and are fully compliant with FAA regulations, including FAA Part 137 certification for agricultural spraying.

How far in advance should I book?

For standard operations, 2-3 days is typical. For urgent requests or specialized applications, call immediately to discuss availability. Weather and seasonal demand may affect scheduling.
