## MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE

### DAILY (AFTER EACH FLIGHT)
- Visual inspection of airframe
- Propeller inspection
- Motor inspection
- Battery inspection
- Spray system cleaning
- Tank flushing

### AFTER EVERY 10 FLIGHT HOURS
- Detailed airframe inspection
- Motor bearing check
- Propeller replacement (or as needed)
- Battery health assessment
- Spray system deep clean
- Calibrate weighing sensors

### AFTER EVERY 50 FLIGHT HOURS
- Complete system diagnostic
- Firmware updates
- Compass calibration
- IMU calibration
- GPS module check
- Structural fastener torque check

### ANNUALLY
- Complete overhaul inspection
- Replace wear items
- Update all software/firmware
- Recertify all systems
