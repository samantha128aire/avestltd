## EXEMPTION REQUEST

Avest Ltd respectfully requests exemption from specific provisions of Title 14 of the Code of Federal Regulations (14 CFR) to conduct agricultural aircraft operations using unmanned aircraft systems (UAS). ## EXTENT OF RELIEF

Avest Ltd seeks relief to conduct agricultural operations using unmanned aircraft systems weighing more than 55 pounds (DJI Agras T40, empty weight 50 kg / 110 lbs) under visual line of sight (VLOS) conditions within Class G airspace, operating below 200 feet above ground level (AGL).

## PUBLIC INTEREST

Granting this exemption serves the public interest by:

### 1. ENHANCING AGRICULTURAL EFFICIENCY
- Precision application reduces chemical waste by up to 30%
- Faster treatment of crops during critical pest/disease windows
- Access to terrain difficult or impossible for traditional equipment
- Reduced soil compaction compared to ground-based equipment

### 2. IMPROVING SAFETY
- Eliminates human exposure to agricultural chemicals during application
- Removes pilot from aircraft-related risks (crashes, rollovers)
- Reduces need for manned aircraft operations in low-altitude environments
- Minimizes environmental contamination through precise targeting

### 3. SUPPORTING SMALL AGRICULTURAL BUSINESSES
- Lower operational costs enable services for smaller farms
- Rapid deployment without need for runway infrastructure
- Flexible scheduling around weather and crop conditions
- Accessible technology for family farms and local operators

### 4. ENVIRONMENTAL BENEFITS
- Reduced drift minimizes impact on adjacent properties
- Lower chemical usage per acre treated
- Reduced fuel consumption compared to traditional aircraft
- Decreased noise pollution in rural communities
