KEY DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

PLATFORM SPECIFICATIONS:
  • Platform Elevation: 30-36" above existing roof
  • Platform Width: 84" (full trailer width)
  • Platform Depth: 60-72" usable area
  • Material: Aluminum extrusion (6061-T6, lightweight)
  • Decking: Aluminum safety grating or composite (non-slip)
  • Load Rating: 500+ lbs per area (platform designed for pilot + drone)

STAIR ASSEMBLY:
  • Width: 9" (between stringers)
  • Rise per Step: 7" (standard dimension)
  • Run per Step: 10" (comfortable tread depth)
  • Number of Steps: 8-10 steps
  • Material: ASTM A36 structural steel, fully welded
  • Handrail: 1.5" diameter steel pipe, 36" above treads
  • Load Rating: 500+ lbs per step

LIQUID STORAGE:
  • Primary Storage: Two IBC tanks (275 gal each) = 550 gallons total
  • Mixing Tank: 60-75 gallon cone-bottom tank for metering
  • Recommended Operating Volume: 400-450 gallons (payload safety)
  • Tank Placement: Over rear and mid axles (balanced weight distribution)
  • Tank Dimensions: ~48"L × 40"W × 46"H per IBC

POWER SYSTEM:
  • Generator: 6 kW portable (120V/240V)
  • Location: V-nose compartment (front-left)
  • Pump: Electric diaphragm or centrifugal (10-15 GPM)
  • Outlets: Two 120V duplex outlets on platform (GFCI protected)
  • Exhaust: 2.5" stainless steel pipe, exits through right-side wall

SAFETY SYSTEMS:
  • Upper Railing: 1.5" diameter pipe, 36" above deck
  • Lower Rail: 1.5" diameter pipe, 12" above deck (kick protection)
  • Mesh Screen: 1/2" or 3/8" holes, 24"H protection zone
  • Anchor Points: Four corners, 500+ lbs load rating each
  • Protective Box: Removable aluminum frame, quick-release pins

TRAILER INTEGRATION:
  • Trailer Length: 186" (163" box + 23" V-nose)
  • Trailer Width: 84" exterior
  • Interior Height: 83"
  • Payload Capacity: 4,700 lbs available
  • Connection: Welded to roof beams via 1/2" gusset plates (4 corners)

================================================================================
SAFETY & COMPLIANCE
================================================================================

CRITICAL SAFETY NOTES:
  ⚠ Confined Space Risk: Generator exhaust MUST exit through right wall
  ⚠ Load Capacity: Platform rated for pilot + drone (~200 lbs max additional)
  ⚠ Electrical Hazard: All circuits require GFCI protection
  ⚠ Wind Loading: DO NOT operate in sustained winds >20 mph
  ⚠ Tie-Down: Platform and drone MUST be secured during transport

STANDARDS REFERENCED:
  • ASTM A36: Structural Steel
  • AWS D1.1: Structural Welding Code - Steel
  • NFPA 70: National Electrical Code (NEC)
  • ANSI A10.48: Safety with Mobile Elevated Work Platforms

INSPECTIONS RECOMMENDED:
  • Structural Engineer Review (gusset weld design, load path)
  • Electrical Inspector (GFCI, bonding, conduit sizing)
  • Welder Certification (AWS D1.1 structural steel)
  • Plumbing Inspector (pressure test and hose certification)

================================================================================
MATERIALS & SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

STRUCTURAL STEEL:
  • Trailer Frame Beams: 1" × 1.5" ASTM A36
  • Stair Stringers: 2" × 4" rectangular tube, 3/16" wall
  • Handrails: 1.5" diameter Schedule 40 pipe
  • Gusset Plates: 1/2" thick ASTM A36 plate
  • All Welds: E7018 (SMAW) or ER70S-6 (FCAW)

PLATFORM FRAME:
  • Extrusions: 2" × 4" aluminum (6061-T6 alloy)
  • Fasteners: Stainless steel bolts/rivets
  • Decking: Aluminum safety grating or composite
  • Finish: Powder coat (satin or textured, UV-resistant)

PLUMBING & ELECTRICAL:
  • Hose: 1-1.5" food-grade polyurethane
  • Pipe: 2-2.5" stainless steel (exhaust)
  • Conduit: 1" flexible metal conduit
  • Wire: 10 AWG copper (generator circuits)
  • Outlets: GFCI-protected 120V duplex

================================================================================
MAINTENANCE & INSPECTION SCHEDULE
================================================================================

ANNUAL:
  • Welds: Visual inspection, dye penetrant testing if cracks suspected
  • Fasteners: Torque verification, replacement if loose
  • Hose/Fittings: Pressure test (50 PSI), replace if leaks found
  • Trailer Tires: Pressure check, tread depth, alignment

6-MONTH:
  • Mesh Screen: Check for corrosion, replace if rust present
  • Protective Box Seals: Check weather degradation, reseal if necessary

PER MANUFACTURER:
  • Generator: Oil changes, filter replacement, fuel stabilization
