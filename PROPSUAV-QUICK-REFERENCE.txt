KEY DESIGN DECISIONS
================================================================================

✓ PLATFORM ELEVATION (30-36"):
  Why:     Optimal pilot eye level for drone visibility & control
  Trade:   Requires substantial structural steel but maximizes safety
  Result:  Superior operational precision

✓ STAIR PLACEMENT (Rear-left corner):
  Why:     Driver's side (left) avoids right-side door interference
  Result:  Stairs in safe zone (outside liquid tanks)

✓ WELDED STEEL STAIRS (vs. bolt-on):
  Why:     Permanent durability, no maintenance, higher load rating
  Result:  Integrated with trailer frame, no loosening fasteners

✓ DUAL IBC TANKS (Over axles):
  Why:     Optimal weight distribution, centers 4,600+ lbs on suspension
  Result:  Minimizes tongue weight imbalance, improves towing stability
  Note:    Full capacity (550 gal) exceeds 4,700 lbs → recommend 400-450 gal max

✓ ALUMINUM PLATFORM (vs. steel):
  Why:     Reduces weight (~200 lbs vs. 500 lbs for steel)
  Result:  Improves payload margin for liquid & equipment

✓ RIGHT-SIDE EXHAUST EXIT (vs. rear):
  Why:     Rear exit would block drone storage; right-side maintains access
  Result:  Exhaust vents away from pilot area (on center-front platform)


SAFETY REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

⚠️  CRITICAL SAFETY ITEMS:

1. CONFINED SPACE HAZARD
   → Generator exhaust MUST exit through right-side wall
   → Operator must use platform in open air ONLY
   → DO NOT operate in enclosed areas (risk of toxic fumes)

2. WIND LOADING
   → Platform acts as sail (84"W × 30"H visible profile)
   → DO NOT operate in sustained winds >20 mph
   → Platform may become unstable; deploy wind brakes if needed

3. ELECTRICAL HAZARD
   → All 120V circuits MUST have GFCI protection
   → Generator bonding required (safety ground connection)
   → Wet environment risk (liquid spraying, water from tanks)

4. TIRE & BRAKING
   → Loaded trailer: ~5,200 lbs (exceeds many small vehicles)
   → Verify towing vehicle rated for 8,000+ lbs combined
   → Verify trailer brakes are functional (longer stopping distance ~250 feet @ 60 mph)
   → Maintain 150+ feet following distance on highways

5. TRANSPORT SECURITY
   → Platform & drone MUST be secured with ratchet straps (4 minimum)
   → Use four anchor points (corners of protective box)
   → Verify tie-downs before towing (visual inspection)

6. LOAD CAPACITY
   → Platform rated for pilot + drone (200 lbs max additional equipment)
   → DO NOT exceed this weight without structural re-analysis
   → Verify all equipment is properly secured during flight ops

TROUBLESHOOTING QUICK GUIDE
================================================================================

PLATFORM ASSEMBLY ISSUES:

Issue: Platform rocks or feels unstable
  Cause: Loose welds or fasteners
  Check: Verify all four corner gusset welds are tight
  Fix: Re-weld if cracks found; torque bolts if assembly is bolted

Issue: Stair treads are slippery
  Cause: Smooth steel or contamination
  Fix: Apply non-slip coating (aluminum oxide tape or grit)

Issue: Protective box won't latch
  Cause: Misalignment during assembly
  Fix: Check hinge alignment; adjust pins or brackets


PLUMBING ISSUES:

Issue: Pump won't prime (no water flow)
  Cause: Air lock or suction line leak
  Check: Verify IBC drain valve is open; listen for sucking sound
  Fix: Loosen suction line fitting, let water drain (burps air), retighten

Issue: Slow flow at nozzle
  Cause: Clogged filter or low tank level
  Check: Verify mixing tank is full; inspect filter (remove trash)
  Fix: Replace filter element; refill tank

Issue: Pressure gauge reads zero (should show 10-20 PSI)
  Cause: Gauge malfunction or pump not running
  Check: Verify pump is running (listen for motor); check gauge valve
  Fix: Replace gauge if confirmed faulty; verify 240V power to pump


ELECTRICAL ISSUES:

Issue: 120V outlets not working
  Cause: GFCI breaker tripped or generator off
  Check: Press "reset" button on GFCI outlet; verify generator is running
  Fix: Check for water/moisture in outlet area (hazard); correct condition before re-arming

Issue: Generator is running but outlets show no voltage
  Cause: Generator output breaker off or faulty
  Check: Locate generator output breaker (should be in ON position)
  Fix: Switch to ON; if trips immediately, generator may be overloaded (check amp draw)

Issue: Pump motor doesn't start
  Cause: 240V circuit breaker off or loose connection
  Check: Locate circuit breaker (should be in ON position); inspect connections
  Fix: Switch breaker ON; if trips again, check for short circuit (call electrician)


WEATHER & ENVIRONMENTAL ISSUES:

Issue: Generator exhaust smell detected on platform
  Cause: Right-side wall exit blocked or wind direction issue
  Check: Inspect exhaust exit for debris; check wind direction
  Fix: Clear obstruction; reposition trailer to vent away from platform

Issue: Platform is wet/slippery after rain
  Cause: Water accumulation on composite decking
  Check: Inspect for standing water or poor drainage
  Fix: Improve drainage; apply non-slip coating; allow air dry before use
