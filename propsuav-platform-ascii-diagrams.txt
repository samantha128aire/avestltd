DIAGRAM 8: STANDARD OPERATING SEQUENCE
================================================================================

PRE-FLIGHT SETUP (15-20 minutes)

1. POSITION TRAILER
   ├─ Park on level ground
   ├─ Engage trailer brakes
   ├─ Deploy stabilizer jacks (if equipped)
   └─ Check wind speed (< 20 mph safe zone)

2. GENERATE POWER
   ├─ Start generator (check fuel level)
   ├─ Allow 5 minutes warm-up
   ├─ Verify 120V/240V output (multimeter test)
   └─ Confirm no exhaust odor inside (right-wall exit working)

3. FILL MIXING TANK
   ├─ Open IBC drain valve #1
   ├─ Start pump (listen for prime)
   ├─ Fill cone-bottom tank to 75% capacity
   ├─ Close drain valve
   └─ Monitor tank level gauge

4. PREPARE PLATFORM
   ├─ Climb stairs (observe safe tread spacing)
   ├─ Unlock protective box (quick-release pins)
   ├─ Position drone on platform deck
   ├─ Secure with ratchet straps to anchor points
   └─ Attach mesh screen (protect from wind-blown debris)

5. LOAD DRONE & BATTERIES
   ├─ Connect battery to charger (on platform electrical outlet)
   ├─ Perform preflight drone checks
   │  ├─ Gimbal & camera
   │  ├─ Propeller inspection
   │  ├─ GPS lock
   │  └─ Spray pump pressure test
   └─ Fill drone spray hopper (from nozzle on platform)

6. FINAL SAFETY CHECK
   ├─ Verify railing integrity (no gaps)
   ├─ Test mesh screen integrity (no tears)
   ├─ Confirm platform is level (bubble level)
   ├─ Check for loose tools or objects
   ├─ Verify generator exhaust clear (visual inspection)
   └─ Brief team on flight path and safety perimeter

IN-FLIGHT OPERATIONS

7. PILOT STATION
   ├─ Stand in center-front area (pilot position)
   ├─ Hold controller at chest height
   ├─ Visual line of sight maintained
   ├─ Spotter positioned at safe distance
   └─ Monitor spray tank pressure (manometer visible on platform)

8. FLIGHT PATTERN
   ├─ Execute programmed spray pattern
   ├─ Monitor drone altitude & GPS
   ├─ Check remaining battery (return to platform @ 30% reserve)
   └─ Observe wind conditions continuously

9. RETURN & REFUEL
   ├─ Land drone safely on platform center
   ├─ Stop all rotors completely before approach
   ├─ Refill spray hopper from platform nozzle
   ├─ Swap battery or charge in-place (generator powering charger)
   └─ Repeat flight pattern if needed

POST-FLIGHT SHUTDOWN (10 minutes)

10. SECURE PLATFORM
    ├─ Power down drone completely
    ├─ Disconnect battery and store in case
    ├─ Lock protective box around drone
    ├─ Secure mesh screen (latches)
    └─ Clear any debris from platform

11. SHUT DOWN POWER SYSTEMS
    ├─ Close IBC drain valves
    ├─ Stop pump (turn off 240V breaker)
    ├─ Drain mixing tank if remaining spraying is done
    ├─ Stop generator (allow cool-down, 2 minutes idle)
    └─ Turn off all 120V outlets

12. SECURE TRAILER FOR TRANSPORT
    ├─ Release stabilizer jacks
    ├─ Verify all hatches/doors closed
    ├─ Verify platform tied down (check ratchet straps)
    ├─ Check trailer lights and brakes
    ├─ Verify tire pressures (205/75R15 @ proper PSI)
    └─ Ready for towing
