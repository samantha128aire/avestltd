Pilot FAQs

Q: What is an Anthropic API key?

A: It's your personal access code to Claude — Anthropic's AI. You create a free account at console.anthropic.com, add a credit card, and we do the rest. You'll only be charged for what you use.

Q: Is my API key safe?

A: Your key is stored securely on our Mac Mini in Hilltop Lakes, TX — never on a cloud server. We use it only to route your iMessage conversations through Claude. You can revoke or regenerate it at any time from your Anthropic console.

Q: What happens after 90 days?

A: You can upgrade to a full AvestAI system (with $295 credit if within 30 days), continue on a month-to-month basis, or we cleanly close out your session. Your data is yours — we'll give it back to you.

Q: Is this the same AI as the full system?

A: Yes, same underlying AI. The difference is the full system sits on your own hardware in your home and has deeper integrations (email, calendar, smart home, etc). The Pilot is iMessage-only — think of it as a taste of what's possible.

Q: What if I decide not to buy a full system?

A: No problem. The Pilot is valuable on its own. You keep using it for up to 90 days. No pressure, no hard sell.

Q: How quickly can I get started?

A: Same day in most cases. Fill out the form, we'll be in touch within a few hours, and you could be texting your AI by tonight.
