## Lessons Learned

**Aviation Safety Education:**
Living at an airpark with a young child means propeller awareness is CRITICAL. This isn't optional safety content — it's life-or-death. The episode needed to be added before the finale to show growth and maturity.

**AI Video Production Consistency:**
Platforms like MagicLight.ai don't automatically maintain visual consistency. You need:
- Detailed character/environment descriptions (Production Bible)
- Reusable prompt structure (Template)
- Explicit prohibitions (no jets, no military aircraft)
- Reference images from successful renders

**Business Decision Framework:**
When evaluating subscription services, test first:
- Monthly > Annual for new tools
- Test 1-2 months before committing to year
- Ensure platform can deliver on specific requirements
- Have clear success criteria (consistency, quality, workflow efficiency)
