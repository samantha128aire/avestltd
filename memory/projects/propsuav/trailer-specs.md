# PropsUAV Spray Trailer Specifications

## Exterior Dimensions
- **Length:** 163" (13'7") + 23" V-nose = 186" total (15'6")
- **Width:** 80"
- **Wheel base:** 2 axles (tandem)

## Interior Dimensions
- **Width:** 80"
- **Height:** 83" (floor to existing roof)
- **Length:** 163" (excluding V-nose interior space)

## Roof Structure
- **Type:** Composite sheet (not suitable for direct foot traffic)
- **Support:** 1" × 1.5" steel beams, 24" on-center spacing
- **Status:** NOT load-bearing for personnel/equipment; requires platform structure

## Ramp & Access
- **Rear ramp:** Single door, 76"W × 80"H
- **Right side door:** 32"W × 68"H, begins 15" from front corner
- **Hitch:** Bumper pull, 2 5/16" ball connection

## Wheels & Fenders
- **Tires:** 205/75R15 (each axle, dual wheels)
- **Fender protrusion:** 9" beyond tire edge
- **Fender length:** 66" per side
- **Stairway width constraint:** Must fit between fender edges (~58-62" available)

## Interior Framing
- **Beams:** 1" × 1.5" steel, 24" on-center spacing
- **Available attachment points:** Ceiling beams run longitudinally
- **Load capacity:** Beams suitable for platform anchorage

## Payload Capacity
- **Gross Vehicle Weight:** 7,000 lbs
- **Trailer dry weight:** ~2,300 lbs
- **Available capacity:** ~4,700 lbs (for equipment + platform + launch deck)

## Equipment to Accommodate
- Spray tank (estimated 25-50 gallons = 150-300 lbs)
- Pump & plumbing
- Generator (battery charging, estimated 200-300 lbs)
- T50 Drone & batteries (estimated 50-100 lbs)
- Launch platform & stairs
- Safety railing system

---

**Design Parameters:**
- Platform elevation: 30-36" above existing roof
- Pilot standing area: Front-center of platform
- Access: Narrow aluminum stairway along fender
- Safety: Railing + mesh protection from drone
- Materials preference: Lightweight aluminum, modular, cost-effective
- Ventilation: Generator exhaust through bottom/side to exterior
