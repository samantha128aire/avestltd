## 🎯 Key Specifications

**Platform Design:**
- Elevation: 30-36" above existing roof
- Width: 84" (full trailer width)
- Launch area: Front-center pilot position
- Stairs: 9" wide welded steel, 10 steps, left fender access
- Safety: Railing + mesh drone protection

**Interior Layout:**
- Two 275-gallon IBC tanks over axles
- 60-75 gallon cone-bottom mixing tank
- Generator in V-nose for battery charging
- Rear area for drone storage/transport
- Exhaust through right-side wall

**Weight & Capacity:**
- Existing trailer: 2,300 lbs
- Available capacity: 4,700 lbs
- Recommended liquid: 400-450 gallons (safe margin)
- Platform + stairs estimate: 550-700 lbs

**Budget Estimate:**
- Total cost: $11,770–$20,400
- Timeline: 9-14 weeks
