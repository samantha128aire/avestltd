## ⚠️ Critical Notes

### Payload Warning
**Full liquid capacity (550 gallons) exceeds the 4,700 lbs trailer payload.**
- At full capacity (550 gal), total weight = ~5,225 lbs (exceeds capacity by ~500 lbs)
- **Recommended Maximum Operating Volume:** 400-450 gallons (~3,500 lbs liquid)
- This allows 1,200 lbs margin for platform, generator, drone, operator, and fuel

### Structural Verification Required
Before construction begins:
1. Inspect existing trailer roof beams for corrosion, cracks, or delamination
2. Verify roof beam sizing (assumed 1"×1.5" steel, 24" spacing)
3. Confirm welds on existing frame are sound (no pre-existing damage)
4. Consult structural engineer if trailer differs from standard enclosed configuration

### Safety Requirements
- ⚠️ Generator exhaust MUST exit through right-side wall (operator safety)
- ⚠️ DO NOT operate in sustained winds >20 mph (platform stability)
- ⚠️ GFCI protection required for all 120V circuits
- ⚠️ Verify trailer brakes functional before transport (loaded weight requires adequate braking)
- ⚠️ Secure platform and drone with ratchet straps during transport (tie-down required)
