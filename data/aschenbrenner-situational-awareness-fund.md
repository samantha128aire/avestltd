## 🧠 Investment Thesis Summary

Aschenbrenner believes AGI arrives ~2027. His portfolio bets on the **physical infrastructure bottleneck**, not the chip designers:

1. **Power** — AI data centers need massive, reliable electricity (BE, EQT, SEI, PSIX, BW, LBRT)
2. **Data Centers / Cloud** — GPU compute infrastructure (CRWV, APLD, CORZ, IREN, WYFI)
3. **Networking** — Optical connectivity between GPU clusters (LITE, COHR)
4. **Storage** — AI training/inference data at scale (SNDK)
5. **Semiconductors (optionality)** — Contrarian Intel bet via calls; Tower Semi (TSEM)
6. **BTC Miners → AI Pivot** — Energy assets + facilities repurposable for AI (CIFR, RIOT, HUT, CLSK, BITF, BTDR)
7. **Real Estate** — Data center-adjacent West Coast facilities (KRC)

**Key Q4 2025 Moves:**
- 🚀 Massively added CoreWeave (CRWV calls +672%), SanDisk (+817%), Applied Digital (+87%)
- 🚪 EXITED: Nvidia puts, Broadcom puts, TSM puts, Micron puts (~$500M in semis hedges removed)
- 🚪 EXITED: Vistra, Galaxy Digital, Seagate, Western Digital
- 🔄 Intel: Sold all equity → kept calls only (pure optionality play)
