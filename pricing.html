Understanding Ongoing Costs

Your AvestAI setup fee covers hardware and configuration. Your AI assistant also requires API access to language models (the "brain" behind the responses). You have two options:

Option A: Pay-Per-Use API Keys (Recommended)

  • Purchase API credits directly from AI providers
  • Only pay for what you use
  • Typical usage ranges:
    • Light use (occasional questions, daily summaries): $20-50/month
    • Moderate use (regular assistance, email management): $50-100/month
    • Heavy use (constant automation, complex tasks): $100-200/month
    • Business/Power use (intensive operations, multiple projects): $200+/month
  • No usage limits or restrictions
  • We recommend Anthropic Claude for quality and reliability

Option B: Monthly Subscriptions (Limited)

  • Use existing ChatGPT Plus, Claude Pro, etc.
  • May hit usage caps or face restrictions
  • Some providers restrict automation use
  • OpenAI/ChatGPT recently began allowing OpenClaw (unconfirmed)

Provider Flexibility: OpenClaw works with Anthropic (Claude), OpenAI (GPT-4), Google (Gemini), and others. We'll help you choose during setup.

Pricing FAQs

Q: Can I upgrade later?

A: Yes! You can add integrations, custom skills, or monthly support anytime.

Q: What if I'm not satisfied?

A: We offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee. If OpenClaw doesn't meet your expectations, we'll refund your setup fee (minus hardware cost for Standard/Premium).

Q: Do you offer business/team discounts?

A: Yes! Contact us for custom quotes on 3+ setups.

Q: Can I pause my monthly support plan?

A: Yes, you can pause or cancel anytime (no contracts).

Q: What are API costs and why do they exist?

A: OpenClaw is the software that manages your AI assistant, but the actual "thinking" happens through AI language models from companies like Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google. These companies charge for access to their models - either through pay-per-use API keys or monthly subscriptions.

Q: Should I use API keys or a monthly subscription?

A: We strongly recommend API keys. Here's why:

  • No usage limits (scale as needed)
  • More reliable (no risk of account restrictions)
  • Often cheaper for moderate use ($50-100/month typical)
  • Designed for automation tools like OpenClaw

Monthly subscriptions (ChatGPT Plus, Claude Pro) were designed for direct human use and may impose limits, ban automation, or throttle usage. Some providers are becoming more flexible, but API keys remain the safer choice.

Q: How much will API usage cost me monthly?

A: Typical usage ranges:

  • Light use (occasional questions, daily summaries): $20-50/month
  • Moderate use (regular assistance, email management): $50-100/month
  • Heavy use (constant automation, complex tasks): $100-200/month
  • Business/Power use (intensive operations, multiple projects): $200+/month

During setup, we'll help you set usage alerts and budgets so you're never surprised.

Q: Can I switch between AI providers?

A: Yes! OpenClaw supports multiple providers. We recommend Anthropic Claude for its balance of quality, reliability, and cost - but you can switch anytime or use different models for different tasks.

Q: Why do you recommend Anthropic Claude?

A: Three reasons:

  1. Quality: Excellent reasoning and instruction-following
  2. Reliability: Stable API with good uptime
  3. Developer-friendly: Built for tools like OpenClaw

That said, OpenClaw works great with OpenAI GPT-4, Google Gemini, and others. We'll discuss your needs during setup.
