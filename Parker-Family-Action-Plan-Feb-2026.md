### 2. PropsUAV - Drone Crop Spraying Business

**License Requirements (Research Complete):**

#### FAA Licenses Needed:
1. **Part 137 Agricultural Aircraft Operator Certificate**
   - Apply via FAA Flight Standards District Office (FSDO)
   - Requires: Operations manual, proof of aircraft/equipment, pilot certification
   - Timeline: 2-4 months for approval

2. **Part 107 Waiver for Agricultural Operations**
   - Already have Part 107 (Remote Pilot UAS #3932937)
   - Need specific waiver for: Operations over people, night operations, BVLOS (beyond visual line of sight)
   - Apply via FAA DroneZone: faadronezone.faa.gov
   - Timeline: 90-120 days typically

#### Texas State License:
3. **Texas Department of Agriculture (TDA) Commercial Applicator License**
   - Category: Aerial Application (1A)
   - Requirements: Pass TDA exam, provide proof of training
   - Apply: texasagriculture.gov
   - Timeline: 4-6 weeks after exam
