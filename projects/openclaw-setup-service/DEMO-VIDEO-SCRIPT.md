# AvestAI — Zoom Demo Video Script
**"What My AI Assistant Does For My Business Every Day"**

- **Format:** Zoom screen share — Chance's camera (laptop) + Mac Mini AI interface visible
- **Target Length:** 8–12 minutes
- **Upload:** YouTube (AvestAI marketing)
- **Tone:** Warm, genuine, slightly amazed — like showing a friend something cool

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES (Read Before Recording)

- Chance should be dressed neat-casual. Good lighting.
- Mac Mini interface should be open in iMessage or a terminal showing the AI chat.
- Keep reactions natural — if something comes out better than expected, let that show.
- Don't rush the pauses. Silence while the AI responds is visual proof it's real.
- Zoom layout: Chance's face in small corner box, AI interface taking up most of screen.
- Record at 1080p. Keep it one continuous take if possible — authenticity over perfection.
- Each "ACTION" block = Chance either speaks to the AI naturally or types. Either works.
