# AvestAI Marketing Plan - Friends First Strategy

**Created:** February 21, 2026  
**Strategy:** Bootstrap with warm network, develop workflow, then scale  
**Target:** 2 customers/month ($40k Year 1)  
**Budget:** $0 (pure bootstrap)

---

## Philosophy: Start Close, Then Expand

**Why friends/network first:**
- ✅ Instant trust (they already know you)
- ✅ Honest feedback (they'll tell you what's wrong)
- ✅ Forgiving customers (they'll be patient during early kinks)
- ✅ Word-of-mouth engine (they'll refer others if it works)
- ✅ Testimonials (easier to ask friends for reviews)
- ✅ Zero marketing cost
- ✅ Perfect for developing your workflow before scaling

**Goal for Month 1:** 2-4 sales from personal network + refine setup process

## First Customer Workflow (Refine This!)

### **Initial Contact → Sale:**
1. Discovery call (30 min) - understand needs, explain service
2. Send Terms of Service + Intake Form
3. Answer questions
4. Close sale (50% deposit)

### **Setup Process:**
1. Order Mac Mini (or customer ships theirs)
2. Install OpenClaw, configure integrations
3. Test everything (remote access, integrations, basic commands)
4. Send remaining payment request
5. Ship Mac Mini or provide remote access credentials
6. Onboarding call (30 min) - walk through usage
7. 30-day support period begins

### **Post-Setup:**
1. Check in at Day 3, Day 7, Day 14, Day 30
2. Ask for testimonial (Day 5-7)
3. Ask for referrals (Day 14-21)
4. Offer monthly support option (Day 25)

**Document what works.** After each customer, update your workflow notes.

## Objection Handling (Friends Version)

### **"That's a lot of money"**
*"I totally get it. Here's the breakdown: $599 Mac Mini + about 20 hours of my time if you did it yourself. I'm charging $1,195 ($100 off for you). That's $596 for my labor, or about $30/hour. Most people value their time at $50-100/hour, so it actually saves money."*

### **"I'm not sure I need it"**
*"Fair enough. Let me ask: How much time do you spend per week on email, calendar, and admin tasks? Most people say 5-10 hours. If AI could cut that in half, you'd save 2-5 hours/week. Over a year, that's 100-250 hours. Worth $1,195?"*

### **"What if it breaks?"**
*"I include 30 days support, and I'll be available after that for hourly support if needed. Plus, you know me - I'm not going anywhere. I'll take care of you."*

### **"Can I just do it myself?"**
*"Absolutely! If you enjoy technical setup and have 20+ hours to spare, DIY is great. I'm offering this for people who'd rather pay someone they trust to do it right the first time."*

### **"I'll think about it"**
*"No pressure. Take your time. If you want to chat more, here's my Calendly link. And the friend discount is good anytime you're ready."*
