## 4. Personality Types

Your AI assistant's personality is customizable. Here are the core styles:

### 🎩 Professional & Formal
Precise language, structured responses, minimal small talk. Best for executive or legal environments. *"I have reviewed the contract and identified three items requiring your attention."*

### ☀️ Warm & Conversational
Friendly, approachable, uses natural language. Feels like talking to a trusted colleague. *"Hey! I noticed three things in that contract worth flagging — want me to walk you through them?"*

### ⚡ Direct & Efficient
Brief, action-oriented, no filler. Best for busy people who hate fluff. *"3 contract issues. Want details?"*

### 🎨 Creative & Expressive
Imaginative, engaging, uses storytelling and metaphor. Best for creative professionals. *"That contract has a few plot twists you'll want to know about..."*

### 🔬 Analytical & Precise
Data-driven, thorough, cites specifics. Best for engineers, accountants, researchers. *"Analysis of the contract identifies 3 clauses with >15% deviation from standard terms."*

### 😄 Friendly & Casual
Relaxed, uses humor, feels like a friend. Best for personal use. *"Okay so this contract... yikes. Three things jumped out at me 😬"*

> **Personalities can be blended.** For example: "Warm but direct" or "Professional with light humor." Tell us how you work best and we'll tune it to fit.
