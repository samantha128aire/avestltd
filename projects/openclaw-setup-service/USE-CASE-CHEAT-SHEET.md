# Use Case Cheat Sheet - For Sales Conversations

**Purpose:** Quick reference for talking to prospects  
**How to use:** Read their profile, identify pain points, share 2-3 relevant examples  
**Print this and keep handy for discovery calls**

---

## CONVERSATION FRAMEWORK

### Discovery Questions (Ask First):
1. "What does a typical morning look like for you?"
2. "What tasks eat up most of your time?"
3. "If you could automate one thing, what would it be?"
4. "Do you feel like you're constantly playing catch-up?"
5. "How do you currently manage [email/calendar/tasks]?"

### Then match their pain to use cases below.

---

## AVIATION PROFESSIONALS

### PILOTS (Commercial, Private, CFI)

**Common Pain Points:**
- Weather briefing takes 20-30 minutes every flight
- Juggling multiple weather sources (Aviation Weather, ForeFlight, NOAA)
- Maintenance tracking across logbooks
- Flight planning repetitive and time-consuming
- Student scheduling chaos (CFIs)

**Use Cases to Share:**

**"Automated Weather Brief"**
- AI checks weather at 6 AM every day, sends personalized brief to your phone
- Includes NOTAMs, TFRs, fuel prices, and go/no-go recommendation
- Real pilot saves 2+ hours/week, never misses critical alerts
- *Close:* "Imagine showing up at the airport already fully briefed"

**"Maintenance Reminder System"**
- AI tracks your aircraft maintenance calendar
- Reminds you 30 days before inspections due
- Logs your flights automatically if you want
- *Close:* "Never stress about missing an annual or AD again"

**"Flight Planning Assistant"**
- Text your AI: "Plan flight from KBOS to KJFK tomorrow 10 AM"
- It checks weather, files flight plan, calculates fuel, checks TFRs
- You review and approve — it does the grunt work
- *Close:* "Cut your flight planning time in half"

**"CFI Student Manager"**
- AI monitors all student calendars
- Sends weather-based go/no-go decisions
- Reschedules automatically when weather scrubs
- Preps lesson materials based on student progress
- *Close:* "One CFI increased billable hours 30% with this"

## ENTREPRENEURS / BUSINESS OWNERS

### SMALL BUSINESS OWNERS

**Common Pain Points:**
- Email overload (200+ unread)
- Calendar chaos (double-bookings, missed meetings)
- Task list that never gets done
- Drowning in admin work
- Can't find time for actual business growth

**Use Cases to Share:**

**"Morning Brief"** ⭐ (Most popular)
- Every morning at 7 AM, AI sends you a text
- Includes: urgent emails, today's calendar, top 3 tasks, industry news
- Plus: AI suggests 3 things it can do FOR you that day
- *Close:* "Real customer saves 7 hours/week. Starts day with clarity, not chaos"

**"Second Brain"**
- You're driving and have a great idea
- Text your AI: "Remember: build feature X for customer Y"
- Later, search "ideas from last week" — instantly find it
- *Close:* "Never lose another brilliant idea. Everything searchable forever"

**"Email Digest"**
- AI reads all your newsletters automatically
- Sends one weekly digest with only the valuable insights
- *Close:* "Consultant went from 200+ unread emails to inbox zero. Stays informed in 10 min/week"

**"Personal CRM"**
- AI monitors your email and calendar automatically
- Tracks last contact with everyone
- Reminds you: "You haven't talked to John in 6 months — he referred 3 clients last year"
- *Close:* "Relationships are your business. This makes sure you never drop the ball"

## REMEMBER

1. **Ask, don't tell.** Discover their pain first, then share relevant use case
2. **Use real examples.** "A pilot I know..." or "Real customer saves 7 hours/week..."
3. **Keep it simple.** Don't overwhelm with tech details. Focus on results.
4. **Paint the picture.** "Imagine waking up to a text with everything you need to know..."
5. **Tie to money.** "At $50/hour, 7 hours/week = $350/week value. Pays for itself in 4 weeks."
