## Sales Script (Phone/Video Call)

### Opening (Build Rapport)
*"Hi [Name], thanks for taking my call. I saw you [found us through Facebook/are a pilot/run a business]. I wanted to walk you through how OpenClaw can save you hours every week. Do you have 10 minutes?"*

### Discovery (Ask Questions)
1. *"What's your biggest frustration with managing your day-to-day tasks right now?"*
   - Listen for: email overload, calendar chaos, forgetfulness, lack of time

2. *"Have you tried AI assistants like ChatGPT or Siri?"*
   - Listen for: "Siri is useless," "ChatGPT forgets everything," "Too much manual typing"

3. *"Walk me through a typical morning. What's the first thing you check?"*
   - Listen for: email, weather, calendar, messages — all things OpenClaw handles

4. *"If you could wave a magic wand and have one thing automated, what would it be?"*
   - Listen for: Specific use case to highlight

### Presentation (Tailored to Their Answers)
*"Here's how OpenClaw solves that..."*

**For Email Overwhelm:**
*"Every morning, OpenClaw reads your inbox, summarizes important emails, drafts replies to routine messages, and flags anything urgent. You wake up to a briefing, not 100 unread emails."*

**For Calendar Chaos:**
*"OpenClaw monitors your calendar 24/7. If someone emails 'Can we meet Thursday at 2?' it checks your availability and drafts a response. No more double-bookings or 'let me check my calendar' emails."*

**For Memory Issues:**
*"Unlike ChatGPT, OpenClaw remembers everything. Your preferences, your contacts, your past conversations. It learns what matters to you and proactively reminds you. Certificate renewal coming up? You'll get a heads-up two weeks before."*

**For Privacy Concerns:**
*"Here's the key difference: OpenClaw runs on YOUR Mac Mini, in your home or office. Your emails, calendar, messages — all stay on your device. Nothing goes to some cloud server. You own the hardware, you own the data."*

### Handling Objections

**"Why not just use ChatGPT?"**
*"Great question. ChatGPT is powerful, but it forgets you every time you close the tab. OpenClaw remembers your life, your preferences, your schedule. It works 24/7 in the background — checking email at 3 AM, monitoring weather, managing your calendar while you sleep. ChatGPT just sits there waiting for you to ask it something."*

**"I'm not technical enough for this."**
*"That's exactly why we exist. You don't set anything up. We pre-configure everything, test it, and hand it to you ready to go. During our onboarding call, I'll walk you through how to use it — it's as simple as texting. If you can use iMessage, you can use OpenClaw."*

**"$1,295 seems expensive."**
*"I get it. Let's break it down: A Mac Mini alone is $600. We're charging $695 for setup. That's 15-20 hours of expert configuration, testing, and customization. If you hired someone at $50/hour to do this, you'd pay $750-1,000 just for labor — and they probably wouldn't do it right. Plus, you get 30 days of support. No monthly fees after that. Compare that to hiring an assistant at $3,000/month. Our service pays for itself in the first week."*

**"What if it breaks or I need help?"**
*"30 days of support included — email, phone, or video call anytime. After that, we offer a monthly plan for $75-150 if you want ongoing support, but most customers don't need it. We also monitor your system for security issues and proactively reach out if something needs attention."*

**"How is this different from Siri?"**
*"Siri is great for 'Set a timer' or 'What's the weather?' But can Siri read your emails, draft responses, check your calendar, and send you a morning briefing? No. OpenClaw integrates with everything — your Gmail, Google Calendar, iMessage, HomeKit, even your flight planning apps. It's like having Siri's smarter, more capable sibling."*
