## Business Summary

### The Offer
Pre-configured Mac Minis with OpenClaw personal AI assistant, fully set up and ready to use.

**Pricing:**
- **$1,295** — New Mac Mini + full setup + 30 days support
- **$695** — Setup customer's existing Mac Mini (saves $600)
- **60 days support** for friends

### Target Market
- Pilots (aviation scheduling, weather, maintenance)
- Entrepreneurs (email overload, calendar chaos, task management)
- Apple enthusiasts (want ecosystem to "just work")
- Agriculture professionals (weather, equipment, market data)

### Unique Value Proposition
- **Privacy-first:** Runs locally on customer's hardware, not cloud-dependent
- **Turnkey:** Pre-configured, tested, ready to use (no 20+ hours of DIY setup)
- **Personal service:** Boutique experience, not corporate tech support
- **Apple ecosystem expertise:** Deep integration with iMessage, iCloud, HomeKit, etc.
- **Security monitoring:** Weekly reports to Samantha for proactive maintenance

## Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| OpenClaw breaking changes | High | Test updates first, maintain staging system, build buffer time |
| Customer dissatisfaction | Medium | Clear expectations, 30-day support, underpromise/overdeliver |
| Data breach | Catastrophic | Never store credentials, temp Apple ID method, cyber insurance |
| Scaling challenges | High | Cap at 2-3/month initially, raise prices as throttle, hire when needed |
| Competition | Medium | Specialize in niches, build reputation early, focus on service quality |
