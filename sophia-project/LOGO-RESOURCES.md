# Logo & Graphics Resources for Sophia Project

## ✅ 1. EAA Logo (FOUND!)

**Official Source:** https://www.eaa.org/eaa/eaa-chapters/eaa-chapter-resources/resources-from-hq/verified-eaa-logos

**Available Formats:**
- EPS (vector - best for editing)
- PNG (high-resolution transparent)
- JPG (solid background)

**Download Links:**
- **Blue & Gray (recommended):** https://www.eaa.org/-/media/Images/EAA/Chapters/resources/EAA_logo_2color-png.png
- **Blue only:** https://www.eaa.org/-/media/Images/EAA/Chapters/resources/EAA_logo_blue-png.png
- **Vector (EPS):** https://www.eaa.org/-/media/Images/EAA/Chapters/resources/EAA_logo_2color-eps.eps

**Usage Rules (per EAA):**
- Must show ® symbol
- Cannot modify colors
- Must leave white space around logo equal to height of "A" in EAA
- Non-commercial educational use is permitted

## ✈️ 3. Piper J-3 Cub Illustration (SEARCHING...)

**Aircraft Specs:**
- Classic yellow "Cub Yellow" (Pantone 1235 C)
- High-wing monoplane (NOT biplane)
- Tandem seating (2-seat)
- Taildragger configuration
- Simple, iconic silhouette

**Where to Find:**
### Free/Low-Cost Options:
1. **Pixabay / Pexels** - Free stock vectors
2. **Vecteezy** - Free vector graphics (attribution required)
3. **OpenClipart** - Public domain
4. **Wikipedia Commons** - Public domain aircraft diagrams

### Paid Options:
1. **iStock / Shutterstock** - Professional vectors ($10-30)
2. **Creative Market** - Aviation illustration packs
3. **Etsy** - Custom aviation art

### DIY Option:
- Trace from photo reference
- Simplify to silhouette style
- Keep it recognizable but stylized for children's book aesthetic
